Rules

  • Control the RED pad.
  • Hit with either ends of the pad to increase the ball speed.
  • Hit using the middle portion to decrease the ball speed.
  • Set the difficulty before starting.
  • Click 'PLAY' anytime to restart or wait and loose :P
  • Refreshing the page or swithcing to another webage will reset the game.

Difficulty settings

  • Easy           -  FPS : 50 | Target Score : 5
  • Medium     -  FPS : 60 | Target Score : 5
  • Pro            -  FPS : 75 | Target Score : 5 | Player pad size decreased.
  • Impossible -  FPS : 90 | Target Score : 3 | Player pad size decreased.
